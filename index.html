About Us

Greetings from Shakti Infotech Pvt. Ltd. (SIPL)

It has been a continuous endeavor of SIPL, since its inception in 2003, to deliver advance solutions across the spectrum from accounting through intelligence. TSPL’s ready-to-deploy product through SIPL’s offerings are scalable, flexible and easy to customize to specific requirements – further allowing integration with any existing system. Currently, SIPL’s product range includes “Tally.ERP 9” – a sophisticated Business Solution Software, and “Shoper 9” for the Retail Industry.

SIPL has continuously developed comprehensive Vocational Training Programs to enable learners gain employable skills in the fields of Business Solutions, Basic Computing Skills, English, & Communication.

SIPL, through Collaborative Programs, partners with Governments, Universities and Autonomous Educational Institutes to integrate vocational training with the mainstream, thereby developing employable skills.
